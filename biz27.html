Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to help you plan your next landscape investment.

Are you licensed and insured for structural landscaping?
Yes. Sydney Landscaping Pty Ltd holds Fair Trading NSW structural licences (Licence No. 375427C) and carries comprehensive public liability and workers compensation insurance.
Do you provide design services or work from plans provided?
We offer in-house concept and documentation packages, but also collaborate seamlessly with architects or designers if you already have plans.
How soon can you start once we approve the quote?
Lead times vary seasonally, but most projects commence within 3–6 weeks after deposit and approvals. We provide a detailed timeline so you can plan ahead.
Can you manage council approvals if required?
Absolutely. Our team helps prepare documentation, liaise with certifiers, and coordinate engineers to keep paperwork painless.
Do you offer maintenance after construction?
Yes, we provide tailored maintenance visits for turf care, pruning, feeding, and irrigation monitoring to protect your investment.
What payment options are available?
We accept EFT, major credit cards, and staged progress payments aligned with milestones. Larger projects can be structured with progress draws.
