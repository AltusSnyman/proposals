HVAC Questions, Answered

Still unsure about timing, pricing, or technology? We’ve got you covered.

Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Our technicians hold current ARCtick refrigeration licenses, CM3 safety accreditation, and full public liability insurance so every job meets Australian standards.
Do you offer emergency HVAC repairs?
Absolutely. Our duty team is on-call 24/7 for urgent breakdowns across Sydney. We arrive with stocked vans to restore cooling or heating fast.
Which areas do you service?
We cover the Sydney CBD, Inner West, Eastern Suburbs, North Shore, Western Sydney, and Sutherland Shire with technicians based locally for faster response.
How do you price installations?
After a site assessment we provide a fixed quotation covering equipment, labor, ductwork, smart controls, and any electrical upgrades required.
Do you provide maintenance plans?
Yes. Our preventative plans include seasonal inspections, filter changes, and priority scheduling to extend system lifespan and maintain warranties.
What brands do you work with?
We install and service Daikin, Mitsubishi Electric, Fujitsu, ActronAir, and other leading brands, always recommending the best-fit for your project.
Can you help improve indoor air quality?
Definitely. We offer filtration upgrades, UV purification, and humidity control solutions to reduce allergens and pathogens in residential and commercial spaces.
How soon can you start?
Most repairs are scheduled same-day. Install projects are typically booked within 3-7 business days depending on equipment availability.
