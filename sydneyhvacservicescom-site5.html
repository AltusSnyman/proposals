FAQ

Answers to Common HVAC Questions

Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Sydney HVAC Services holds ARCtick refrigeration licensing, public liability, and workers’ compensation coverage. Certificates are provided with every quote.
Do you provide emergency support after hours?
We maintain an on-call technician 24/7 for critical commercial and residential outages. Average arrival time is 45 minutes within metro Sydney.
What brands do you work with?
We install and service Daikin, Mitsubishi Electric, Panasonic, Fujitsu, ActronAir, and most major commercial brands. Manufacturer training keeps us current.
Do you offer free quotes?
Absolutely. Site inspections and proposal drafting are complimentary for new installations and maintenance agreements.
How often should systems be serviced?
Residential systems benefit from annual servicing, while commercial equipment typically requires quarterly visits to stay compliant and efficient.
Do you help with energy efficiency upgrades?
Yes. We conduct load calculations, thermostat automation, and retrofit existing systems with variable-speed technology to lower power bills.
