Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before booking with Integrity Hedging & Gardens.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We hold full public liability insurance, white card accreditation and are police-cleared to work on residential and strata properties.

How soon can you attend new jobs?

Most hedge and maintenance bookings are scheduled within 7–10 days. For urgent tidy-ups, we’ll do our best to squeeze you in sooner.

Do you offer free quotes?

Absolutely. We provide free on-site consultations so we can measure accurately and tailor the right solution for your property.

What happens to green waste?

Every visit includes full clean-up and removal of clippings, branches and lawn waste unless you prefer mulch left on site.

Do you design new gardens or just maintain?

We do both. From planting plans and turf installs to feature lighting and pathways, our landscaping team can handle the full scope.

How often should hedges be trimmed?

Most hedges thrive with trims every 6–8 weeks during the growing season. We’ll set a calendar that fits your species and growth rate.

Do you help with reticulation repairs?

Yes, from finding leaks to installing smart controllers, we diagnose and repair all common irrigation issues while keeping systems compliant.
