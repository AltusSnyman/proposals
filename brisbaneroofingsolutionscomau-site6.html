FAQs

Roofing Questions We Hear Daily

Can’t find your answer? Call (07) 3276 1546 and talk directly to a roofer.

Do you really offer free onsite inspections?+
Yes. A licensed roofer visits your property, walks the entire roof, takes photos, and provides a detailed quote—no obligation and no need for you to supply pictures.
What areas do you serve in Queensland?+
We regularly service Brisbane North & South, Ipswich, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, and Toowoomba. For larger commercial projects we travel across South East Queensland.
How long does a full roof restoration take?+
Most tiled roofs are completed in 4–6 days, depending on size and weather. We provide a schedule before work begins and keep you updated each day.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Absolutely. Brisbane Roofing Solutions holds QBCC Licence 15030347 and full public liability insurance up to $20M for residential and commercial works.
Do you offer financing or staged payments?+
Yes, we partner with Handypay to provide flexible payment plans for restorations and replacements, subject to approval.
What warranty do you provide?+
All workmanship carries a 10-year guarantee. Paint/coating systems come with manufacturer warranties up to 15 years depending on product selection.
Can you work with insurance claims?+
Yes. We assist with storm damage reports, provide photographic evidence, and liaise with insurers to ensure your claim moves smoothly.
Do you replace gutters and install whirlybirds?+
We supply and install Colorbond gutters, downpipes, fascia repairs, and whirlybirds as standalone projects or bundled with roof work.
