Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about roof restorations, timelines, and our guarantees.

Do you really offer a free onsite inspection?+
Yes. We believe accurate pricing only comes from walking the roof. Our team inspects every surface, takes photos, and provides a detailed report at no cost.
How long does a full roof restoration take?+
Most tile restorations take 3–4 days depending on weather and roof size. We schedule consecutive days and keep you updated if rain delays occur.
What products do you use?+
We use premium Dulux AcraTex and Shieldcoat membranes formulated for Queensland UV conditions, backed by manufacturer warranties.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Absolutely. We hold QBCC Licence 15030347, public liability insurance, and adhere to all workplace safety standards.
Do you offer financing?+
Yes, through our partner Handypay. We can walk you through simple repayment plans during your quote appointment.
Which areas do you serve?+
We service Brisbane (north & south), Ipswich, Logan, Redlands, Moreton Bay, the Sunshine Coast, Toowoomba, and the Gold Coast.
Do you handle small repairs too?+
Yes, from single broken tiles to flashing upgrades and whirlybird replacements, no job is too small.
What maintenance do you recommend after restoration?+
We recommend a roof health check every 18–24 months and clearing gutters each storm season to extend the life of your coatings.
