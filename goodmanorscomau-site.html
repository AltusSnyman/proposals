FAQs

Answers to common questions

How do your design fees work?
We typically begin with a fixed-fee concept package covering site analysis, 3D visuals, and documentation for approvals. Upon approval, fees transition into construction contracts with clear allowances.
Are you licensed builders?
Yes. GOODMANORS holds NSW Building Licence 39086 and carries full insurance for structural, pool, and landscape works.
Do you handle approvals and engineering?
We coordinate DA/CDC documentation, liaise with private certifiers, and bring in structural and hydraulic engineers as required.
What is the typical timeline?
Concept design generally runs 6–8 weeks. Construction durations range from 12 to 26 weeks depending on complexity and weather.
Can you work with my architect?
Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with architects and interior designers to ensure continuity across the entire property.
What maintenance support do you offer?
Our Care program covers horticultural upkeep, pool chemistry, equipment servicing, and seasonal refreshes to keep everything flourishing.
