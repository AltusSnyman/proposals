Landscaping FAQs

Answers to common questions about working with Sydney Landscaping Pty Ltd.

Are you licensed and insured for structural landscaping?

Yes. Licence No. 375427C covers structural works statewide and we carry full public liability and workers comp insurance.

Do you provide design-only services or construction only?

We can do design-only masterplans, construction-only if plans are ready, or the full design-build package for seamless delivery.

How soon can you start a new project?

Lead times range from 3-6 weeks depending on scope and approvals. We book site consultations within 7 days.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes, initial consultations and preliminary estimates are complimentary. Detailed design packages are quoted before commencement.

What areas across Sydney do you cover?

We regularly service the Northern Beaches, North Shore, Hills District, Inner West, and select projects across Greater Sydney.

Can you coordinate pools, lighting, and irrigation in-house?

Absolutely. Our licensed team handles pool surrounds, lighting design, and Hunter/Netafim irrigation systems internally.

What payment schedule do you require?

Typically 10% deposit, progress payments at key milestones, and balance upon completion after walkthrough approval.

Do you provide aftercare or maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer 6 and 12-month maintenance packages including seasonal pruning, fertilising, and system checks.
