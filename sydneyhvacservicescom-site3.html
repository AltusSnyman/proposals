HVAC Questions Answered

Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes. Sydney HVAC Services holds ARCtick refrigeration licensing, electrical endorsements, and comprehensive public liability insurance for every technician on site.
Do you charge for quotes? +
No. We provide free, itemised quotes across Sydney including site inspections, load assessments, and system recommendations.
How fast can you respond to emergencies? +
Our dispatch monitors calls 24/7 and typically gets a technician to metro Sydney addresses within 90 minutes for urgent breakdowns.
What brands do you service? +
We maintain and install all major brands including Daikin, Mitsubishi Electric, ActronAir, Fujitsu, Temperzone, and more without voiding warranties.
Do you offer maintenance plans? +
Yes. Our Priority Protection Plan provides quarterly visits, compliance paperwork, and 10% discounts on repairs for a flat monthly fee.
Which areas do you cover? +
We service the entire Greater Sydney region including CBD, Inner West, North Shore, Northern Beaches, Parramatta, and the Shire.

We accept EFT, major credit cards, and can arrange staged payments for larger installation projects.
