FAQ

Roofing Questions, Answered

Do you really offer free onsite inspections?
Yes. We physically inspect every roof at no cost so we can measure accurately, identify hidden issues, and provide fixed pricing—not guesses from photos.
How long does a roof restoration take?
Most residential restorations are completed in 3–5 days depending on repairs and weather. We keep you updated daily and protect your property at every stage.
Are you licensed and insured?
We hold QBCC Licence 15030347 and carry full public liability insurance. All crew members are trained in height safety and workmanship standards.
What payment options are available?
We accept EFT, major credit cards, and offer flexible Handypay finance so you can spread payments with approvals in under a minute.
Do you service both tiles and metal roofs?
Absolutely. We restore concrete tiles, terracotta, Colorbond, and custom metal roofs, tailoring products to each material for maximum lifespan.
How quickly can you start?
Quotes are typically scheduled within 3 business days. Restoration start dates are usually within 2–3 weeks, with priority slots for leak repairs.
